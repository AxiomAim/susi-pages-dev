About SUSI Adapters, Inc.

Founded in 1995 by engineer and inventor Chris Matthews, SUSI Adapters, Inc. changed the way electric meters were installed by inventing and developing the Flush Mount socket adapters and retrofit adapters for the electric meter industry. That transformed meter replacements from matching the old existing meter to, “Any meter for any panel with SUSI Adapters”, which created tremendous flexibility (in vendor, cost and features) for the end user. He asked the simple question “What do you have and what do you want?” then figured out how to make that happen.

Over the last 25 years, SUSI Adapters has grown the business with partnerships including 15 meter manufacturers. It’s a win for SUSI, a win for the partner and a loss for the companies that don’t join us. This business philosophy came from his days playing Monopoly where he coined the phrase “Win-Win-Lose”.

Now SUSI Adapters has 10 series of products, 100’s of specific products and 1000’s of installed products at Utility, industrial and commercial facilities in the USA and around the world.

We now have manufacturing facilities in Georgia and Texas.
